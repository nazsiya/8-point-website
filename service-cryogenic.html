MULTI-LAYER VAPOR BARRIERS

Cryogenic insulation failure is almost always a result of moisture ingress. Our proprietary multi-layer system utilizes zero-permeance vapor membranes interleaved with rigid PIR/PUR sections to ensure a continuous thermal break and moisture exclusion zone.
